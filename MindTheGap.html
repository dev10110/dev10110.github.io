Mind The Gap

Mind The Gap is a smartphone app I developed during my internship with HackPartners, over the summer of 2020.

The app was designed to aid with social distancing. It can measure the distance between devices over 2 meters away with an accuracy of 8.5 cm.

I specifically worked on designing the audio processing stack and designing the algorithm to allow for a scalable application.

I also wrote a paper detailing the technical aspects of the application, available at Arxiv.
